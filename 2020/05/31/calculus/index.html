一条线在三维空间中的表示方法:

vector equation:
$$
\vec r = \vec r_0 + t\vec v,\ t\in R
$$
parametric equation:
$$
x=x_0+at,\ \ y=y_0+bt,\ \ z=z_0+ct, \ \ t\in R
$$
symmetric equation:
$$
t=\frac {x-x_0} a=\frac {y-y_0}b=\frac{z-z_0}c
$$


三维空间中的线可以表示为
$$
\vec r = \vec r_0 + t\vec v,\ t\in R
$$
因此可以把它看作是一个对于t的函数

vector-value function (vector function):
$$
r(t) = <f(t),\ g(t),\ h(t)> = f(t)i+g(t)j+h(t)z
$$
f(t), g(t), h(t)是real-value function, 叫做 component function (or coordinate function) of r(t)



Limit
$$
\lim_{t->a}r(t)=<\lim_{t->a}f(t),\lim_{t->a}g(t),\lim_{t->a}h(t)>
$$

Continuity:

The vector function r(t) is continuous at t=a if
$$
\lim_{t->a}r(t)=r(a)
$$

Derivative:

The vector function r(t) is differentiable at t=a if the following limit exists:
$$
\frac {dr} {dt}|{t=a}=r’(a)=\lim{\triangle t->0} \frac {r(a+\triangle t) - r(a)}{\triangle t}
$$

同时我们也可以证明:

$$
\frac {dr}{dt} = r’(t)=<f’(t),g’(t),h’(t)>=f’(t)i+g’(t)j+h’(t)k
$$


Definite Integral

Integration of vector-value functions is also defined as for real-value functions:
$$
\int_a^b r(t)dt :=\lim_{n->\infin}\sum_{i=1}^nr(t_i^*)\triangle t
$$


Indefinite Integral

向量函数 R(t) 是r(t)的反导数 antiderivative, 如果R’(t) = r(t)
$$
\int r(t)dt=R(t)+\vec c
$$

$$
\vec c=c_1 i+c_2j+c_3k
$$


Fundamental Theorem of calculus


证明: r’(t) 与 r(t) 正交


Vector Functions

Arc Length

如果一条线在三维空间中表示为
$$
r(t) = <f(t),\ g(t),\ h(t)>
$$
Arc Length就是曲线的长度

也就是在每一个点的导的模的和
$$
L=\int_a^b||r’(t)||dt=\int_a^b \sqrt {f’(t)^2+g’(t)^2+h’(t)^2} dt
$$

同理也可以拓展到高维空间中
$$
L=\int_a^b||r’(t)||dt
$$


Arc Length Function

我们定义arc length function s为
$$
s(t)=\int_a^b||r’(t)||dt=\int_a^b \sqrt {f’(t)^2+g’(t)^2+h’(t)^2} dt
$$
因此s(t)是 r(a) 到 r(b) 之间curve的长度

如果两面同时求导
$$
\frac {ds}{dt}=|r’(t)|
$$

It is often useful to parametrize a curve with respect to arc length, because arc length arises naturally from the shape of the curve and dose not depend on a particular coordinate system.

If a curve r(t) is already given in terms of a parameter t and s(t) is the arc length function. then we can solve t as a function of s. t = t(s). then r = r(t) = r(t(s)).

Thus, if s=3, r(t(3)) is the position vector of the point 3 units of length along the curve from its starting point.



Curvature

A parametrization r(t) is called smooth on interval I if r’ is continuous and r’(t) != 0 on I

如果C是一个smooth curve 定义by vector function r, unit tangent vector T(t) is given by:
$$
T(t)=\frac {r’(t)}{|r’(t)|}
$$
就是曲线每一点的切线的单位向量.


curvature of C at a given point is measure of how quickly the curve changes direction at that point. Specifically we define it to be the magnitude of the rate of change of the unit tangent vector with respect to arc length.

(We use arc length so that the curvature will be independent of the parametrization.) Because the unit tangent vector has constant length, only changes in direction contribute to the rate of change of T.

The curvature of a curve is
$$
k=| \frac {dT} {ds}|
$$
T is the unit tangent vector.

就是切线随着曲线长度的变化速率. dT是切线的变化, ds是曲线长度的变化.

$$
k=| \frac {dT} {ds}|=|\frac {dT/dt}{ds/dt}|
$$

而 ds/dt = r’(t), (在Arc Length Function下的结论)

所以
$$
k(t) = \frac {|T’(t)|}{|r’(t)|}
$$

证明: 半径为a的圆的curvature为1/a
$$
r(t)=<a\cos(t), a\sin(t)>
$$

$$
r’(t)=<-a\sin(t),a\cos(t)>
$$

$$
T(t)=\frac {r’(t)}{|r’(t)|} =<-\sin(t),\cos(t)>
$$

$$
T’(t) = <-\cos(t),-\sin(t)>
$$

$$
k(t) = \frac {|T’(t)|}{|r’(t)|}=\frac {1}{a}
$$

半径越小的圆的curvature越大.

而一条直线的曲率为0, 因为tangent vector(T(t))为常数, 所以它的导就是0, k(t)就为0


the curvature of the curve given by the vector function r is
$$
k(t)=\frac {|r’(t) \times r’’(t)|}{|r’(t)|^3}
$$

证明:

由于T=r’/|r’| 同时 |r’|=ds/dt 所以
$$
r’=|r’|T=\frac {ds}{dt}T
$$
两边同时求导
$$
r’’=\frac {d^2s}{dt^2}T+\frac {ds}{dt}T’
$$
由于T x T = 0
$$
r’\times r’’=\frac {ds}{dt}T\times \frac {ds}{dt}T’ = (\frac {ds}{dt})^2 (T\times T’)
$$
由于|T(t)|=1 for all t, and T and T’ are orthogonal
$$
|r’\times r’’|=(\frac {ds}{dt})^2 |T\times T’|=(\frac {ds}{dt})^2 |T||T’|=(\frac {ds}{dt})^2 |T’|
$$
因此:
$$
|T’|=\frac {|r’\times r’’|}{(ds/dt)^2}=\frac {|r’\times r’’|}{|r’|^2}
$$

$$
k=\frac {|T’|}{|r’|}=\frac {|r’\times r’’|}{|r’|^3}
$$

The radius of curvature is
$$
p=\frac 1k
$$


The Normal and Binormal Vectors

在一个平滑的曲线r(t)上的一点. 有许多向量都与unit tangent vector T(t)正交

我们选择T’(t)的单位向量, 因为T(t)与T’(t)正交

因此我们定义, at any point where k!=0, we can define the principal unit normal vector N(t) [or simply unit normal] as
$$
N(t)=\frac{T’(t)}{|T’(t)|}
$$
我们认为unit normal vector指出了 curve 在每个点变化的方向


$$ B(t)=T(t)\times N(t) $$ B(t)叫做binormal vector. 它与T 和N都正交, 同时它也是一个单位向量, 因为T和N都是单位向量


对于curve C来说, 在点P能求出T, N 和 B

由N和B组成的平面叫做normal plane, 它与T正交

由T和N组成的平面叫 osculating plane, It is the plane that comes closest to containing the part of the curve near P. (For a plane curve, the osculating plane is simply the plane that contains the curve.)


The circle that lies in the osculating plane of C at P, has the same tangent as C at P. lies on the concave side of C(toward which N points), and has radius p = 1/k (the reciprocal of the curvature) is called the osculating circle(or the circle of curvature) of C at P. It is the circle that best describes how C behaves near P; it shares the same tangent normal and curvature at P.

A function f of two variables is a rule that assigns to each ordered pair of real numbers (x, y) in a set D a unique real number denoted by f(x,y). The set D is the domain of f and its range is the set of values that f takes on, that is {f(x,y) |(x, y) ∈ D}

The level curves of a function f of two variables are the curves with equation f(x,y)=k, where k is a constant (in the range of f)

Higher Derivative

fxy意味着先对x求导在对y求导. 或者表达为
$$
f_{xy}=\frac {\part^2f}{\part y \part x}
$$

Clairaut’s Theorem

Suppose f is defined on a disk D that contains the point (a,b). if fxy and fyx are both continuous on D, then
$$
f_{xy}(a,b)=f_{yx}(a,b)
$$


Partial Differential Equation

partial differential equation在物理学中有重要应用

$$
\frac {\part^2u}{\part x^2} + \frac {\part^2u}{\part y^2}=0
$$

这个式子叫做 Laplace’s equation(拉普拉斯方程). Solutions of this equation are called harmonic functions. they play a role in problems of heat conduction, fluid flow, and electric potential.



$$
\frac {\part^2u}{\part t^2}=a^2\frac {\part^2u}{\part x^2}
$$

这个式子叫做wave equation

描述了波的运动: 海浪，声波，光波或沿振动弦传播的波

例如如果 u(x,t) 表示振动的小提琴琴弦在 某个时间 t , 一端到弦的距离x, 的位移, 那么 u(x,t)就满足wave equation. Here constant a depends on the density of the string and on the tension in the string.



涉及三个变量的函数的偏微分方程在科学和工程中也非常重要。 三维拉普拉斯方程为
$$
\frac {\part^2u}{\part x^2} + \frac {\part^2u}{\part y^2}+\frac {\part^2u}{\part z^2}=0
$$
